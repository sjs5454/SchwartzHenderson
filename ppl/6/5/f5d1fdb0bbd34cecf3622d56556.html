Paula's Notes:

Joseph Svartman was a tobacco farmer in Bessarbia (sometimes Russian, sometimes Polish)
Came from Neporostovo, Russia
Came to US 1906 brought son Harry to US in 1908. Went back to Russia to get balance of family in 1913 and was trapped by WWI (1914).
In 1921 Harry brought entire family from Russia including one brother (coud this be David??) and3 sisters and mother and father.
In 1930 lived at 158th Street & Grand Concourse with wife, son Abraham, daughter Clara with her son Isidor Roiter
In 1910 census may have lived son Harry (21) , immigrated in 1909 and brother or cousin ) Abraham 52 who immigrated in 1908(fur operator) as boarders in Manhattan.101 Stanton Street. Was a presser in fur industry
Name may be Schwartzman??? Svartman on manifest Marriange record of son shows Josef Shwartzman
